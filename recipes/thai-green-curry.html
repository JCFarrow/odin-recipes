Thai Green Curry

A mildly spicy chicken curry. One of my favorites.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup coconut cream
  • 2 tablespoons green curry paste
  • 150 grams sliced chicken breast
  • 1 1/2 cups coconut milk
  • 200 grams eggplant (bite sized pieces)
  • 20 grams small eggplant
  • 2-3 kaffir lime leaves
  • 2 teaspoons palm sugar (or brown sugar)
  • 1 to 1 1/2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1/2 large red chili (thinly sliced)
  • 40 grams (1/2 cups) sweet basil leaves
  1. Boil coconut cream over low heat until oil appears
  2. Add curry paste and cook 1 minute until fragrant
  3. Add chicken and cook for 1 minute
  4. Add coconut milk
  5. Bring to boil and add eggplant and kaffir lime leaves
  6. After eggplant becomes soft, add sugar, fish sauce and chili
  7. Add basil at last moment and stir
