Massaman Curry

A delicious Thai curry with a rich blend of flavors. One of my favorites.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups coconut cream
  • 2 tablespoons massaman curry paste
  • 200 grams beef/chicken/lamb (3 cm chunks)
  • 50 grams onions (3 cm chunks)
  • 200 grams boiled potatoes
  • 1 1/2 cups coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon palm sugar (or brown sugar)
  • 1 1/2 to 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1-2 tablespoons tamarind puree
  • 4 roasted cardamon pods (crushed)
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2-4 cm cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons roasted peanuts

Steps

  1. Boil 1 cup of coconut cream in wok over low heat until some oil rises to the surface. Then add curry paste and fry for 1 min until fragrant.
  2. Add meat, onion and potato. Keep stirring until meat is nearly cooked.
  3. Add coconut milk and bring to boil.
  4. Add palm sugar, fish sauce, tamarind, cardamon, bay leaves and cinnamon.
  5. Simmer on low until meat is tender.
  6. Add remaining coconut cream and peanuts.
  7. Cover with lid and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  8. Serve with rice.
